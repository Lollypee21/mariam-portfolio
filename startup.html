Registered 2017 | Offa LGA, Nigeria

Empowering youth with practical entrepreneurial skills in production of essential daily needs, fostering self-reliance and economic independence.

Vision

To become a leading hub in Cosmetology production, as well as a leading driver of youth entrepreneurship in Nigeria by promoting self-employment
